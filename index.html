About Us

"आपकी कला आपको पहले,समाज बादमे बदलती है"

CARV-Hindi or Circle of acting at RV is the dramatics Society of RV College of Engineering, Bengaluru. CARV-Hindi is a distinguished name throughout the country known for it's legacy of producing some of the best Street plays.We aim to captivate interest, We help you discover the art that's burried deep inside. We have a lot to showcase as achivements, But all those are just additonal benefits of the journey we all are on. What CARV Hindi Promises is a Journey, A Journey with a family that believes in the artist inside.

Divisions

The four pillars.The wheels of our vehicle.Them Specialists.

ACTING

“"रिशते में तोह हम तुम्हारे बाप लगते हैं, नाम है शहंशाह!"”

Acting is the art of communicating with the audience by the means of expressing. They are the ones who connect to the audience and become the heroes and villains of common people. They make us cry, laugh and motivated at the same time by playing Hitler to Mahatma Gandhi to Charlie Chaplin!

MUSIC

“एक प्यार का नगमा है”

A person maybe the performer, but music is the soul of any performance. Vocalists, and various instrument players which bring music enhance the experience of action, suspense, drama, comedy, tragedy and life.

TECHNICAL

“परंपरा, प्रतिष्ठा, अनुशासन ”

Technical work is controlling the final presentation of any performance. He/She can edit out people, merge emotions and make smiles come on every face who watches it. It includes learning new softwares and marketing skills in addition to having a lot of fun

WRITING

“गोली नहीं मारेंगे, हम कह के लेंगे ”

A person who writes is a writer. A writer of CARV Hindi can see his/her characters come to life and take shape, fulfilling the story in the imagination and turn a poetry into an emotion.
